Lee Kuan Yew

"If you want to reach your goals and dreams, you cannot do it without discipline"

THE LIFE AND TIMES OF LEE KUAN YEW

1923

Born on 16th of September to Lee Chin Koon and Chua Jim Neo

1935

Attended the Raffles Institution

1947

Studied Law at Fitzwilliam College, University of Oxford

1950

Admitted to the English Bar

1954

Became Secretary-General of People's Action Party (PAP)

1959

Sworn in on 5th of June as Prime Minister of newly-independent Singapore

1963

Led a referendum movement and merged Singapore with the Federation of Malaysia

1965

Signed an agreement to remove Singapore from the Federation of Malaysia

1990

Resigned from his position as Prime Minister of Singapore

1992

Stepped down as Secretary-General of the People's Action Party

2015

Died at the age of 91 on the 23rd of March due to severe pneumonia
